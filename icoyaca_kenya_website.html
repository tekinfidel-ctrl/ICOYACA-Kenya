Who We Are
About ICOYACA Kenya

ICOYACA Kenya is the Kenya Country Chapter of the Independent Continental Youth Advisory Council on AfCFTA (ICOYACA), an independent body affiliated to YouLead Africa. We were established to ensure that Kenya's young people are meaningfully represented in, and benefit from, the implementation of the African Continental Free Trade Area.

Kenya is both a gateway economy for East and Central Africa and one of the continent's most dynamic entrepreneurial ecosystems. Yet the voices of young Kenyan traders, entrepreneurs, and policy advocates are often absent from the corridors where trade rules are made and implemented.

ICOYACA Kenya changes that. We mobilise young Kenyans — from Nairobi's tech corridors to Mombasa's port economy, from Kisumu's lake-basin traders to Eldoret's agribusiness youth — to engage with AfCFTA implementation, build trade capacity, and shape Kenya's continental trade agenda.

Our Mission

To empower Kenya's youth to meaningfully engage in and benefit from AfCFTA implementation through advocacy, capacity building, research, and bottom-up mobilisation — ensuring that trade integration works for every young Kenyan.

We are guided by the continental ICOYACA framework, aligned to Kenya's national trade and development priorities, and accountable to the young Kenyans we serve.
